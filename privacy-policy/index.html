01

Hordhac

Darkpen waxay xushmeyneysaa asturnaanta isticmaalayaasha. Siyaasaddani waxay sharaxaysaa xogta aan uruurino, sida aan u isticmaalno, iyo xuquuqdaada.

App-ka isticmaalida waxay macnaha leedahay inaad ogolaatay siyaasaddani. Haddaadan ogolaanin, fadlan app-ka ha isticmaalin.

02

Xogta Aan Uruurino

Markaaad account sameyso, waxaan uruurinaynaa:

  • WhatsApp number — waajib (login iyo aqoonsi)
  • Password — waajib, encrypted ayuu ku kaydiyaa
  • Email — ikhtiyaari, password recovery kaliya
  • Chat history — AI chat iyo group conversations
  • Books iyo Exams — gelitaanka taariikh iyo isticmaalka
  • Payment records — subscription lacag bixinta taariikh
  • App usage data — adeegga hagaajinta
03

Sida Aan Xogta U Isticmaalno

  • Account-kaaga u furista iyo ilaalinta
  • AI chat iyo group features u shaqeynta
  • Books iyo Exams gelitaanka maamulida
  • Subscription iyo lacag bixinta xaqiijinta
  • Password-ka lumay hadduu email lagu heliyo
  • App-ka hagaajinta iyo cilladaha xallinta
Muhiim: Xogta aad noo siisay qof kale weligaynu kuma iibinno, una gudbinno. Darkpen xogta adeegga waxqabadka kaliya ayay u isticmaashaa.
04

AI Chat — Gemini API

App-kan AI chat-ku wuxuu isticmaalaa Google Gemini API. Markaaad AI-ga su'aal weydiso:

  • Su'aashaada Google Gemini servers ayaa mareysa
  • Google's Privacy Policy ayaa sidoo kale dhaqmeysa
  • Chat history servers-keena ayuu ku kaydiyaa
  • Xasaasi ama personal ah wax ha AI-ga ku sheegin
05

Lacag Bixinta

Darkpen waxay isticmaashaa lacag bixin manual:

  • Credit card ama bank details aan ku kaydinno
  • Subscription-ka admin ayaa si gacanta ah u xaqiijiya
  • Xaqiijin message ayaad heshaa lacagta la aqbalay
Digniin: Darkpen waxba kuma dalbato card number, CVV, ama bank PIN. Hadduu qof taas ka dalbado — waa xad gudub, noo soo warbixin.
06

Xuquuqdaada

  • Xogta arag — account-kaaga ku jirta xog
  • Xogta saxo — email, profile details
  • Account tirtir — xogta oo dhan permanently la tirtiraa
  • Data export — xogta koobi u codsó
07

Account Tirtirada

Haddaad account-kaaga tirtiri rabto:

  • App fur → Settings → "Delete Account" taabo
  • Xaqiijin ayaa kaa codsanaysa
  • Xogta oo dhan 7 maalmood gudahood permanently la tirtiraa
  • Chat history, payment records, profile — dhammaantood la tirtiraa
Ogeysiis: Account tirtiriddu waa aan la noqon karin. Subscription-ka lacagtii la bixiyay waa la lumiyaa.
08

Carruurta Ilaalinta

Darkpen 18 sano ka yar carruurta looma talagalin. Haddaan ogaano 18 ka yar oo account leh, isla markaa account la xidhaa xogtiisana la tirtiraa.

09

Siyaasadda Isbeddelkeeda

Siyaasaddani waxay isbeddeli kartaa. Isbeddel weyn hadduu dhaco, waxaan ku ogeysiin doonnaa app-ka gudihiisa ama email-kaaga. Isticmaalka sii wadida macnihiisu waa ogolaansho cusub.

01

Introduction

Darkpen respects and protects the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By using the Darkpen app, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

02

Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Darkpen, we may collect:

  • WhatsApp number — required for login and account identity
  • Password — required, stored in encrypted form
  • Email address — optional, used only for password recovery
  • Chat history — AI conversations and group messages
  • Books & Exams — access records and usage history
  • Payment records — subscription and transaction history
  • App usage data — to improve our service
03

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and secure your account
  • To power AI chat and group features
  • To manage access to Books and Exams content
  • To verify and process subscription payments
  • To recover your account if you forget your password
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
Important: We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is used solely to operate the Darkpen service.
04

AI Chat — Gemini API

Darkpen's AI chat is powered by Google Gemini API. When you message the AI:

  • Your message is processed through Google's Gemini servers
  • Google's own Privacy Policy also applies to this data
  • Chat history is stored on our servers for continuity
  • Do not share sensitive personal or financial information with the AI
05

Payments

Darkpen uses a manual payment system:

  • We do not store credit card or bank account details
  • Subscription payments are manually verified by an admin
  • You will receive confirmation once your payment is approved
Warning: Darkpen will never ask for your card number, CVV, or bank PIN. If anyone claiming to be Darkpen requests these — it is fraud, report it immediately.
06

Your Rights

  • Access — view all data stored about you
  • Correct — update your email or profile information
  • Delete — permanently remove your account and all data
  • Export — request a copy of your personal data
07

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  • Open app → Settings → tap "Delete Account"
  • You will be asked to confirm your decision
  • All data permanently deleted within 7 days
  • Includes chat history, payment records, and profile information
Notice: Account deletion is irreversible. Any active subscription credits will be forfeited upon deletion.
08

Children's Privacy

Darkpen is not intended for users under 18 years of age. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will immediately suspend it and delete all associated data.

09

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. For significant changes, we will notify you via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
